Meet Daniela

Blending clinical expertise
with genuine human connection

Daniela Mondello is an Accredited Practising Dietitian and Nutritionist with over 15 years of clinical experience. She holds a Bachelor of Science in Nutrition and Dietetics with 1st Class Honours from the University of Sydney — one of Australia's most prestigious nutrition programs.

Her areas of specialist knowledge span the full metabolic spectrum: weight management, bariatric surgery, hormonal challenges including PCOS and thyroid conditions, insulin resistance, and optimising blood glucose control through dietary changes. She has a genuine passion for educating and coaching others on how to live a happy and healthy lifestyle.

Daniela appreciates the importance of nurturing the dietitian–client relationship and is passionate about using nutrition to manage all parts of the metabolic spectrum. As a working mother herself, she deeply understands the real-world challenges of achieving a good work–life balance — and approaches every client with practicality and warmth, helping you make choices that complement your lifestyle.

Daniela is a proud member of Dietitians Australia and the Health at Every Size community, bringing a non-diet, evidence-based lens to every consultation.
