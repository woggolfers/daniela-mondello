Meet Daniela

Helping you feel your best through good food and a healthy lifestyle

I'm an Accredited Practising Dietitian who is passionate about supporting my clients to improve their health in a way that feels realistic, sustainable and aligned with their lifestyle. I work from a non-diet approach, focusing on building a positive relationship with food and empowering lasting behaviour change.

I have experience supporting a wide range of health concerns, with particular interests in women's health, metabolic health and gut health. I work with conditions such as obesity, diabetes, PCOS, insulin resistance, cardiovascular risk and gastrointestinal conditions — and I have a strong passion for longevity, the Mediterranean diet and the lessons we can learn from the Blue Zones.

I believe that each client is unique, and I take the time to understand your individual goals, challenges and lifestyle before we build a plan together. I'm a registered member of Dietitians Australia and bring a warm, evidence-based, non-diet lens to every consultation.
