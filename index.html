About Daniela

Helping you feel your best through good food and a healthy lifestyle

I'm an Accredited Practising Dietitian passionate about supporting my clients to improve their health in a way that feels realistic, sustainable and aligned with their lifestyle. I work from a non-diet approach, focusing on building a positive relationship with food and empowering lasting behaviour change.

With a deep interest in women's health, metabolic health, gut health and longevity — including the Mediterranean diet and the Blue Zones — I combine clinical expertise with genuine warmth, because sustainable change starts with feeling truly supported.
