Services

Personalised, evidence-based nutrition care across the full metabolic spectrum — in person and via Telehealth.

⚖️

Weight Management

Sustainable, evidence-based approaches to weight loss and long-term weight management. Daniela takes the time to understand your individual circumstances, health history and lifestyle — because no two bodies, or weight journeys, are the same.

🔬

Bariatric Surgery Support

Comprehensive pre- and post-operative nutrition counselling to maximise your surgical outcomes and prevent nutrient deficiencies. Daniela also supports fertility and pre-conception nutrition for those who have had bariatric surgery.

🩺

Diabetes Nutrition

Personalised dietary strategies for Type 1, Type 2 and gestational diabetes — helping you optimise blood glucose control through food, without compromising on enjoyment or quality of life.

🌸

PCOS & Hormonal Health

Targeted nutrition support for polycystic ovarian syndrome (PCOS), thyroid conditions and other hormonal challenges — including the management of insulin resistance, which underpins many of these conditions.

🤰

Pregnancy & Fertility Nutrition

Expert guidance for pre-conception nutrition, pregnancy, post-partum and breastfeeding — including specialist support for fertility and pre-conception health after bariatric surgery.

🫁

Gut Health

Assessment and dietary management of gut health concerns, including IBS, bloating, constipation and other digestive issues — using evidence-based approaches to help restore balance and comfort.

🧠

Relationship with Food & Eating Behaviours

A compassionate, Health at Every Size-informed approach to emotional eating, disordered eating and improving your relationship with food — free from diet culture and without guilt or shame.

💊

Nutrient Deficiencies

Assessment and management of nutrient deficiencies through personalised dietary intervention and practical meal planning — particularly important following bariatric surgery or with certain medical conditions.

What to expect in a consultation

01

Comprehensive Assessment

A thorough review of your diet, medical history, lifestyle and health goals — taking the whole picture into account.

02

Medical Nutrition Therapy

Evidence-based counselling and education tailored to your specific health conditions and circumstances.

03

Personalised Diet Plan

A practical, achievable eating plan designed around your real life — not an idealised version of it.

04

Ongoing Support

Tailored dietary monitoring and follow-up consultations to help you stay on track and adapt as your needs evolve.
