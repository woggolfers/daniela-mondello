Services

Personalised, evidence-based nutrition care across the full metabolic spectrum — in person and via Telehealth.

⚖️

Weight Management & Obesity

Sustainable, evidence-based approaches to weight management — including support for clients on GLP-1 medications. Daniela works with you to build habits that are realistic, enjoyable and lasting.

🩺

Diabetes Nutrition

Personalised dietary strategies for Type 1, Type 2 and gestational diabetes — helping you optimise blood glucose control through food, without compromising on pleasure or quality of life.

🌸

Women's Health

Targeted nutrition support for PCOS, endometriosis, perimenopause and menopause. Daniela understands the unique hormonal and metabolic challenges women face at every life stage.

🫁

Gut Health

Assessment and dietary management of IBS, IBD (Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis), coeliac disease, diverticulitis, reflux and food intolerances — using evidence-based approaches to restore comfort and balance.

❤️

Heart Health

Dietary management of high cholesterol, high blood pressure and cardiovascular disease risk. Daniela has a particular passion for the Mediterranean diet and its evidence-based role in heart health and longevity.

🧬

Insulin Resistance & Metabolic Health

Specialist support for insulin resistance, fatty liver and metabolic syndrome — conditions that sit at the root of many chronic diseases and respond well to targeted dietary intervention.

🧠

Relationship with Food & Eating Behaviours

A compassionate, non-diet approach to disordered eating and emotional eating — focused on building a positive, sustainable relationship with food free from guilt and restriction.

👶

Paediatric Nutrition

Nutrition support for children's growth and development, nutrient deficiencies, and picky or fussy eating — helping families build healthy, positive food habits from an early age.

🥗

General Healthy Eating & Lifestyle

For anyone looking to improve their overall eating habits and energy levels. Daniela works with clients across all life stages to build practical, enjoyable and lasting dietary changes.

🌍

Longevity & Preventive Nutrition

Daniela has a deep interest in longevity, the Mediterranean diet and the Blue Zones — helping clients adopt evidence-based dietary patterns that support a long, vibrant and healthy life.

What to expect in a consultation

01

Comprehensive Assessment

A thorough review of your diet, medical history, lifestyle and health goals — taking the whole picture into account.

02

Medical Nutrition Therapy

Evidence-based counselling and education tailored to your specific health conditions and circumstances.

03

Personalised Diet Plan

A practical, achievable eating plan designed around your real life — not an idealised version of it.

04

Ongoing Support

Tailored dietary monitoring and follow-up consultations to help you stay on track and adapt as your needs evolve.
